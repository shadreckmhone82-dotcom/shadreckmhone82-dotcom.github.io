“AND SUDDENLY”

And suddenly I was made free
Suddenly he paid the fee
For me to be whole again
He took my pain

Suddenly he said let there be light
And then suddenly I started seeing things right
My past held me back for ages
But he changed my story again

He told me all you need is light to shine
All you need to do is be mine
He said and I believed
Here I'm to testify that I'm his child

Then suddenly I felt a weight lift off my chest
The darkness faded, and my soul found rest
The light of his love shone bright and true
And I knew in that moment, I was brand new

He healed my wounds, he calmed my fears
He gave me a voice, and wiped away my tears
He showed me my worth, and my value too
And I knew that I was loved, and that I was true

Then suddenly I stood up tall
With a heart full of joy, and a spirit that would enthrall
I walked in the light, with a newfound might
And I shone with a radiance, that shone so bright

— Gen-esis the Poet
